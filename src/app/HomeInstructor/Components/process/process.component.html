your application process

To become an instructor, follow these simple steps:

Submit Your Application

Fill out our instructor application form, providing details about your expertise, teaching experience (if any), and the course you would like to create.

Course Proposal

Share your course idea, including the learning objectives, syllabus, and any unique features or insights you plan to incorporate.

Review and Approval

Our team will review your application and course proposal to ensure they meet our quality standards.

Onboarding and Support

If your application is approved, you'll receive onboarding materials, guidelines, and access to our instructor resources. We'll also provide ongoing support and assistance throughout your journey as an instructor.
